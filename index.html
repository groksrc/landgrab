What is LandGrab

Landgrab is a community-focused NFT marketplace to buy, sell, and discover the best real estate across the virtual worlds of the metaverse. Our platform provides the best tools to make informed and intelligent data-driven decisions on buying, selling, and renting virtual land in the growing world of play-to-earn blockchain-based gaming.

Why LandGrab

Landgrab will operate differently than other established NFT marketplaces that try to be everything to all people, treating all NFTs in the same general way. The Landgrab marketplace will equip buyers and sellers with the proper tools to evaluate virtual land buying and selling opportunities, with additional powerful features driven by market analytics. For Landgrab, the community is everything for us, and we will devote significant resources to ensure our community is engaged and rewarded for participation in our ecosystem.

TOKENOMICS

Our upcoming ERC20 token will be used to reward community members for marketplace participation and to provide governance rights so the community can decide on the long-term direction for the platform.
